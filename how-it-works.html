HOW DOES SWIMFUN WORK?

If you have a pool and want you or your kids to learn how to swim, then Swimfun is for you!

Swimfun instructors will come to your pool to provide quality lessons.

Spend less and get more, with Swimfun!

Customize the lesson type, lesson length, and number of students to fit your needs.

Lessons can be from 30 minutes to 60 minutes.

Lessons can be up 8 students per instructor.
